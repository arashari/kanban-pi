# Cendol Recipe

Cendol is a refreshing Southeast Asian dessert with pandan jelly noodles, coconut milk, and palm sugar syrup over ice.

## Ingredients

### Pandan Jelly Noodles (Cendol)
- 100g rice flour
- 40g tapioca starch
- 40g mung bean flour (hunkwe)
- 1/2 tsp pandan paste or pandan juice
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 500ml water

### Palm Sugar Syrup
- 250g palm sugar (gula melaka/jawa), chopped
- 200ml water
- 2 pandan leaves, knotted
- 1/4 tsp salt

### Coconut Milk
- 400ml thick coconut milk
- 200ml water
- 2 pandan leaves, knotted
- 1/2 tsp salt

### Toppings
- Crushed ice
- Cooked red beans (optional)
- Glutinous rice (optional)

## Instructions

### Make the Jelly Noodles
1. Mix rice flour, tapioca starch, mung bean flour, and salt in a bowl.
2. Add water gradually while stirring until smooth. Mix in pandan paste for the green color.
3. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens into a smooth translucent paste (about 10-12 minutes).
4. Press the hot mixture through a cendol strainer or potato ricer directly into a bowl of ice water.
5. Let the noodles firm up for 10 minutes, then drain and set aside.

### Make the Palm Sugar Syrup
1. Combine palm sugar, water, pandan leaves, and salt in a saucepan.
2. Simmer over low heat until sugar dissolves and syrup thickens slightly (10-15 minutes).
3. Strain and cool to room temperature.

### Prepare the Coconut Milk
1. Combine coconut milk, water, pandan leaves, and salt in a saucepan.
2. Warm over low heat until just simmering. Do not boil.
3. Remove from heat, discard pandan leaves, and cool.

### Assemble
1. Place green jelly noodles in a glass or bowl.
2. Add red beans if using.
3. Pour in palm sugar syrup (3-4 tablespoons, to taste).
4. Ladle coconut milk over the top.
5. Add crushed ice generously.
6. Serve immediately.

## Tips
- The ice bath is critical so the noodles set quickly and keep their shape.
- Do not boil the coconut milk or it will separate and curdle.
- Components can be made ahead and refrigerated for up to 2 days. Assemble just before serving.

*Serves 4-6*
